• Reschedule a step / revision
    • Abort a step: Click 'abort'. Important: After clicking abort, the state will switch to "aborted", but you must wait until the state has changed to "issues" or "failed" until doing anything else with that step (otherwise the bot will never realize you aborted it).
    • Reschedule a step: Click 'clear'. Don't do this after a revision has completed (i.e. cleaned up), the step will run again and fail (since everything has been deleted).
    • Reschedule a revision: click 'reset work'. Don't do this while a bot is working on the revision (you may abort all steps first to shortcurcuit a bit).
